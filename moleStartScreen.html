Whack-a-Mole

The moles have taken over Grandpa John's beautiful vegetable garden!

Help Grandpa John to get rid of the moles by stepping on them. You have 30 seconds to catch as many moles as possible by stepping on a tile when it lights up. Be careful not to step on tiles that do not light up. Grandpa John doesn't need you stomping on his vegetables.

PS! Catching moles is easier with multiple people ;)

To start, step on any tile or press "start".
